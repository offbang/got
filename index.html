Hybrid Athlete Protocol

A structured weekly routine integrating dumbbell strength training and endurance running.

Strength Training Split

Monday: Chest, Triceps & Core

  • Dumbbell Floor Press: 4 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Overhead Tricep Extensions: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Weighted Abdominal Crunches: 3 sets of 15 reps
Focus: Controlled form, moderate intensity, and deep muscle contraction.

Wednesday: Legs, Shoulders & Calves

  • Goblet Squats: 4 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Dumbbell Overhead Shoulder Press: 4 sets of 10 reps
  • Weighted Calf Raises: 4 sets of 15-20 reps
Focus: Balance, posture, and full range of motion.

Friday: Back, Biceps & Core

  • Dumbbell Rows: 4 sets of 8-10 reps (per side)
  • Bicep Curls: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Weighted Abdominal Holds: 3 sets of 30-45 seconds
Focus: Back engagement and arm isolation.

Endurance Running Plan

Three runs per week spaced between workout days (e.g., Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday) to ensure optimal endurance building and recovery balance.

Run 1 (Recovery/Base)

10 km

Run 2 (Tempo/Pace)

10 km

Run 3 (Long Run)

15 km

The Environment & Philosophy

Setting: Designed for a simple home or outdoor workout setting relying on minimal equipment (two dumbbells and bodyweight).

Lighting & Vibe: Natural, realistic, and motivating. It emphasizes the raw effort and discipline of a true fitness lifestyle.

Execution: The athlete maintains proper form at all times. Progression is gradual, and consistency is the ultimate metric for success.
